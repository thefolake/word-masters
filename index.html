Word Masters

How to Play Word Masters

  • You start by guessing any five-letter word.
  • After you submit your guess, each letter of your word will be highlighted in one of three colors:
    • Green: The letter is correct and in the right position.
    • Yellow: The letter is in the word but in the wrong position.
    • Gray: The letter is not in the word at all.
  • Based on the feedback, adjust your next guess. Use the information from the colored feedback to make a better guess.
  • You have a total of six attempts to guess the correct 5 letter word.
  • If you guess a word that is not a valid word, the row will blink red to indicate that it's incorrect. Use backspace to remove the word and try again with a valid one.
  • If you guess the word correctly within six tries, you win the game!
  • If you don't guess the word within six attempts, you lose, and the correct word is revealed.

Example

  • Suppose the word of the day is "PLANT"
  • If you guess "CRANE":
    • C (Color Gray): Not in the word.
    • R ( Color Gray): Not in the word.
    • A (Color Yellow): In the word but in the wrong position.
    • N (Color Yellow): In the word but in the wrong position.
    • E (Color Gray): Not in the word.
  • You would use this feedback to form your next guess.

Tips

  • Start with a word that has a variety of common letters.
  • Pay attention to the position of letters as you receive feedback.
  • Use the process of elimination for letters that have been grayed out.
